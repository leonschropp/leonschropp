Bachelor Thesis

My bachelor thesis focused on simplicial and semisimplicial sets, Kan extensions, and realization. During this project, I was introduced to model categories and simplicial homotopy for the first time. Although the results presented in the thesis were largely known, it was a good opportunity to get familiar with the world of categories. Feel free to ask if you would like a copy.
