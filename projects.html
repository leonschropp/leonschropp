Master Thesis

I'm currently working on my Master Thesis. A prominent feature will be the etale homotopy type of higher stacks, as introduced by D.Carchedi in this paper. I'm also planning to work out relations of this construction to the sheaf cohomology of \( \infty - \) topoi, aiming for similar results as in the classical case. Also I would like to present a good number of applications, possibly in log geometry. Originally it was my goal to include relations to \( \mathbb{A}^1 \) Homotopy Theory. However, that might be a bit much for a Master thesis. I will upload a short overview here, soon.

Bachelor Thesis

My bachelor thesis focused on simplicial and semisimplicial sets, Kan extensions, and realization. During this project, I was introduced to model categories and simplicial homotopy th for the first time. Although the results presented in the thesis were largely known, it was a good opportunity to get familiar with the world of categories. Feel free to ask if you would like a copy.
