Hi, I'm Leon Schropp

I'm a Master's student in Mathematics at LMU Munich,where I'm writing my Master's thesis under the supervision of Markus Land. My main interests lie in algebraic topology and algebraic geometry. I'm particulary interested in etale and A^1 Homotopy Theory, algebraic K-Theory and derived algebraic geometry. However, I also enjoy reading about more classical topics involving classifications and moduli spaces.
On the side, I work as a Software Engineer at MTU Aero Engines and participate in the transition town initiative Trudering im Wandel.
